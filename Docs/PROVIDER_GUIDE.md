# CareCova Provider Guide
## Healthcare Facility Portal — Complete Reference

---

## Who is a CareCova Provider?

A **CareCova Provider** is a licensed healthcare facility — hospital, clinic, dental practice, or wellness centre — that has been onboarded onto the CareCova platform to offer healthcare financing to their patients.

As a provider, CareCova acts as your **financing partner**. Instead of turning away patients who cannot afford upfront payment, you refer them to CareCova, and CareCova funds the care. The patient repays CareCova in instalments while you receive payment immediately after disbursement.

---

## What Can a Provider Do?

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard Overview** | See your facility's total patients, active loans, total disbursed, repayment rate |
| **Patient List** | View all patients who applied for financing through your facility |
| **Loan Applications** | Track every financing application linked to your facility and its approval status |
| **Repayment Tracking** | Monitor repayments made by your patients on their loans |
| **Register a Patient** | Submit a financing application on behalf of a patient directly from the portal |
| **Shareable Registration Link** | Share a unique link so patients can apply themselves and automatically link to your facility |
| **Facility Profile** | Update your facility's name, type, contact details, and address |

## How Patients Get Added to Your List

There are **three ways** a patient can appear on your patient list:

### 1. Patient Applies via Your Registration Link
Every provider has a unique shareable link displayed on their dashboard:
```
https://carecova.com/apply?providerId=YOUR_ID
```
When a patient opens this link and submits an application, it is **automatically linked to your facility**. No manual action needed.

**How to share it:**
- Log in → Dashboard → copy the link from the blue "Your Patient Registration Link" card
- Share via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or display it on your reception desk as a QR code

---

### 2. Patient Applies via the Public Form and Selects Your Facility
When a patient visits `carecova.com/apply` directly and searches for your facility in the **Hospital / Clinic** field, selecting your facility from the CareCova network automatically links their application to you.

**How it works:**
1. Patient opens the apply form
2. In "Hospital / Clinic Details" → searches for your facility name
3. Selects your facility from the dropdown
4. Submits the application
5. Their application appears on your **Patients** and **Loan Applications** tabs immediately

---

### 3. You Register the Patient Directly
From your portal, you can fill out the application on behalf of a patient.

**Steps:**
1. Log in → click **Register Patient** (top-right on the Patients page)
2. Fill in the 4-step form:
   - **Step 1** — Patient's name, phone, email, BVN (optional), address
   - **Step 2** — Treatment details (category, procedure, urgency, hospital name)
   - **Step 3** — Financial details (employment, income, amount needed, duration)
   - **Step 4** — Patient consent (data processing, terms — patient must agree)
3. Submit — the application is created and the patient appears on your list instantly

> The patient will receive a confirmation and can track their application at `carecova.com/track`

## Getting Started — Provider Onboarding

### Step 1: Your Facility is Created by CareCova Admin
You cannot self-register. A CareCova administrator creates your facility account and sets up your portal login credentials (email + password). You will receive these via email or direct communication.

### Step 2: Log In to Your Portal
Go to `https://carecova.com/provider/login` and enter your email and password.

> If you have forgotten your password or have never logged in before, contact your CareCova account manager to have it reset.

### Step 3: Explore Your Dashboard
After logging in you land on the **Overview** page showing:
- Total patients registered at your facility
- Number of loan applications and their statuses
- Total amount disbursed to your facility's patients
- Repayment rate across all your patients
- Your unique patient registration link

### Step 4: Share Your Registration Link
Copy your registration link from the dashboard and share it with patients who need financing. This is the fastest way to grow your patient list.

### Step 5: Register Your First Patient
If a patient is in front of you and needs financing now, click **Register Patient** and fill in their details. You can complete it in under 5 minutes.

## Portal Pages — Detailed Guide

### Dashboard (Overview)
The first page you see after login. Shows:
- **Total Patients** — everyone who has applied for financing at your facility
- **Loan Applications** — total number of applications with a breakdown by status
- **Total Disbursed** — cumulative amount CareCova has paid out for your patients
- **Repayment Rate** — percentage of due repayments that have been paid on time
- **Registration Link** — your shareable URL with a one-click copy button

---

### Patients
A searchable list of all patients linked to your facility.

**Columns:**
- Patient name and avatar
- Phone number
- Email address
- Loan status (Active, Approved, Pending, Disbursed, Completed, Rejected)
- Date registered

**Actions:**
- **Search** — filter by name or phone number
- **Refresh** — reload the list
- **Register Patient** — open the registration form

---

### Loan Applications
All financing applications associated with your facility.

**Columns:**
- Patient name
- Requested amount
- Approved amount (shown once reviewed)
- Treatment category (Surgery, Dental, Maternity, etc.)
- Application status
- Date submitted

**Filtering:** Use the status filter to view only Pending, Approved, Active, Completed, or Rejected applications.

---

### Repayments
All loan repayment transactions from your facility's patients.

**Summary cards:**
- Total collected
- Pending payments outstanding
- Total number of transactions

**Per-row details:**
- Patient name
- Amount paid
- Payment date
- Payment channel (bank transfer, card, salary deduction, etc.)
- Status (Paid, Pending, Overdue, Failed)

---

### Facility Profile
View and edit your facility's information.

**Editable fields:**
- Facility name
- Facility type (Hospital, Clinic, Dental, Gym/Wellness)
- Email address
- Phone number
- Physical address
- Contact person name

Click **Edit Profile** to make changes and **Save** to confirm.

## Application Statuses Explained

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Application submitted, awaiting review by CareCova team |
| **Approved** | CareCova has approved the loan, awaiting disbursement |
| **Disbursed / Active** | Funds have been released to the patient; loan is active |
| **Completed** | Patient has fully repaid their loan |
| **Rejected** | Application was reviewed and declined |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I reset my portal password?**
Contact your CareCova account manager. They can reset your password from the admin panel.

**Q: A patient says they applied but I can't see them on my list.**
Ask the patient to confirm they either used your registration link or selected your facility by name during the application. If they typed the hospital name manually (custom entry), it will not auto-link. Ask them to re-apply using your link.

**Q: Can I see the loan amount approved for a patient?**
Yes — the Loan Applications tab shows the approved amount once a decision has been made.

**Q: How quickly is a patient's loan disbursed after approval?**
Disbursement is typically processed within 1–3 business days of final approval.

**Q: Can multiple staff members log in to the portal?**
Currently each facility has one portal login account. Contact CareCova to request additional access.

**Q: What happens if a patient doesn't repay?**
CareCova manages the repayment relationship directly with the patient. The facility is not liable for patient defaults.
